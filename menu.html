Our Menu

From tandoori spice kicks to cheesy cheese bursts and ice-cold refreshments, explore our crowd favorites. Fresh, fiery, and completely delicious.

Chef's Special Veg

Tandoori Paneer Tikka Burger

Crispy golden paneer patty smothered in rich tandoori spices, topped with sliced onions, fresh capsicum, and our signature fiery mint chutney mayo.

Crispy Veggie Crunch Burger Veg

A crispy mixed-vegetable patty layered with sliced tomatoes, crunchy lettuce, cheddar cheese, and a creamy house special burger sauce.

Spicy Butter Chicken Burger Non-Veg

Juicy butter chicken fillet cooked in smooth makhani gravy, topped with crunchy pickled onions and a dollop of fresh green herb cream.

Double Cheese Margherita Pizza Veg

Crispy thin crust loaded with double mozzarella cheese, rich Italian san marzano tomato sauce, and topped with fresh basil leaves.

Paneer Tikka & Capsicum Pizza Veg

Juicy paneer tikka cubes, crunchy green capsicum rings, sliced red onions, and mozzarella, drizzled with tandoori mayo.

Chicken Tikka & Jalapeno Pizza Non-Veg

Spicy smoked chicken tikka, fiery jalapeno slices, mozzarella, and dynamic garlic marinara base, baked to a bubbly cheese finish.

Crowd Favorite Non-Veg

Fiery Chilli Chicken Momos

Spicy pan-fried chicken dumplings tossed in our hot garlic chilli paste, green onions, and fresh coriander, served with fiery red chutney.

Classic Steamed Veg Momos Veg

Thin wheat dumplings packed with finely minced cabbage, carrots, beans, and spring onions, steamed and served with mild garlic mayonnaise.

Crispy Fried Paneer Momos Veg

Crunchy golden fried momos stuffed with soft spiced paneer chunks and spices, served with our hot schezwan dip.

Peri-Peri Salted Fries Veg

Crispy golden potato fries generously dusted with spicy African peri-peri spice mix, served with garlic mayo dip.

Cheesy Cheese-Burst Fries Veg

A loaded bucket of crispy fries topped with liquid cheese sauce, melted mozzarella, chopped jalapenos, and green herbs.

Signature

Belgian Chocolate Shake

Rich, ultra-thick milkshake blended with premium imported Belgian cocoa, topped with chocolate fudge, fresh cream, and chocolate flakes.

Kesar Pista Badam Shake Rich Desi

A rich traditional milk blend infused with saffron threads (kesar), crushed pistachios, and chopped almonds, chilled to perfection.

Mango Mastani Shake Seasonal

Thick mango pulp milkshake topped with a scoop of premium vanilla ice cream, glazed tutty-fruity, and chopped pistachios.

Fresh Mint Mojito Classic

Muddled fresh mint leaves, lime wedges, and raw organic cane sugar, topped with sparkling soda water and crushed ice.

Spiced Jamun Spritzer Desi Twist

Tangy black plum (jamun) nectar, a touch of kala namak (black salt), roasted cumin powder, mint, and lemon spritzed with club soda.

Masala Cold Drink Street Style

Your favorite cola beverage upgraded with fresh lemon juice, chaat masala, black salt, and a refreshing sprig of mint.

Pour-Over Coffee Estate Origin

V60 hand-brewed single-origin beans, rotated seasonally. Choose between our light-roast Ethiopian Yirgacheffe (floral/citrus) or Kelagur Estate India (nutty/caramel).

Cortado Bold

Equal parts rich house-blend espresso and warm steamed organic whole milk, serving a perfectly balanced bold flavor.

Rose Cardamom Latte Specialty

Double shot espresso blended with our house-infused rose-cardamom syrup and velvety textured milk, dusted with organic rose petals.

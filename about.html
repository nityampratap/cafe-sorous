The Vibe

Late Nights, Crazy Bites, and Endless Chai-Coffee Addas.

We started Aether & Crumb because we felt Jumeirah needed a real hangout spot. A place where you don't feel rushed, where the music matches the energy, and where the comfort food is loaded with cheese and packed with Desi spices. We're not a corporate franchise—we are a neighborhood café designed for your late-night catch-ups, board game marathons, and story sessions.

The Kitchen

The Tandoori & Cheese Fusion

We are obsessed with that perfect balance between global street food and rich Indian spices. We marinate our paneer tikka slices in a blend of hand-pounded spices for hours before grilling, and we stack our burgers high with molten cheddar and signature green mint mayonnaise.

Whether it's a double-cheese margherita pizza or loaded peri-peri fries, we make sure every single bite hits the spot. No compromises on cheese, no shortcuts on spices.

Desi Comfort

Steaming Hot Momos & Pizza Pulls

Indian street-food culture is incomplete without momos. Our chef curates thin wheat dumplings stuffed with spiced mince, steamed to juicy perfection, or pan-fried in hot garlic chilli glaze. Pair a plate of chicken chilli momos with a bubbly slice of paneer tikka pizza, and you have the ultimate comfort combination.

We believe that food tastes better when shared. That's why our portions are generous and designed for the gang.

The Bar

Overloaded Shakes & Slow Brews

From ultra-thick Belgian chocolate shakes topped with chocolate curls to traditional rich Kesar Pista Badam blends, our beverage menu is made for indulgence. If you want something refreshing, our spiced jamun spritzer brings that nostalgic childhood tang with a refreshing sodafied twist.

And for the coffee lovers, we keep the roasters running. We serve premium Indian and Ethiopian single-origin coffee poured slow over V60 filter setups.
